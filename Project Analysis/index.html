Project Analysis

Client: Wilson’s Barbecue

Project: Website Redesign

Designer: Michael Capanelli

Site Intent

Wilson’s Barbecue is a traditional smokehouse and BBQ restaurant located In Fairfield Connecticut. Wilson’s is a family owned and operated business operated for the love of cooking. That love is translated in to well crafted traditional dishes made with care. The majority of business is from referrals of satisfied customers. There’s a very lived in, simple feel from the storefront to the interior of this family restaurant. While the food is well put together, the website is haphazardly thrown together at best.

The site has a simple background and logo. The navigation is not user friendly at all and the site lacks overall polish and has a serious lack of content to engage a user. I feel that taking the website In to the 21st century would attract a new, younger, more vibrant customer base and help propel Wilson’s to the top of their game.

Goals for Redesign:

  • Rearrange content so it has visual flow
  • Create a useable navigation solution.
  • Add pricing to the site along with an option to order online through the already existing contact form
  • Bring the site to current HTML and CSS standards.
  • Make the site responsive so it will render cleanly on multiple devices

Targeted Audience

The target audience for a restaurant is varied and coves a wide demographic and as such makes zeroing in on a user persona rather difficult. There is no specific age or gender demographic being targeted by Wilson’s. So using the family oriented nature of the restaurant I’ll we’ll look at two very different personas
