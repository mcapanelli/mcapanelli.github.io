Catering

We have multiple catering packages already set up, but if you would like something customized we can make pretty much anything work!

Our three pre-organized packages are listed below:

Ed's Choice- feeds approximately 5-10 people

2lb pulled pork, 2 chickens, 1 slab of ribs, 2 LG potato salad, 2 LG slaw, 1 8oz sauce

Penfield- feeds approximately 10-15 people

3 slabs of ribs, 3 chickens, 1/2 pan of salad, 2 LG potato salad, 2 LG slaw, 1/2 pan of cornbread, 1 8oz sauce

Slaughterhouse- feeds approximately 25-30 people

4lb pulled pork, 4 chickens, 4 slabs of ribs, 1 pan of coleslaw, 1 pan of potato salad, 1 pan of cornbread, 1 16oz sauce
