Instructors

Michael Capanelli

Michael has had a life long love affair with the Marital arts. Starting in Shotokan karate when he was a child, he went on to study Okinawan Goju Ryu and later, European Martial arts. He’s worked at interrupting the manuals under the guidance of Gene Davis, an anthropologist who specials in the era. Michael brings a sense of urgency to his training and always fights with intent.

Brad Rangel

Brad has been at HEMA for three years. Gene Davis, who also introduced Brad to Michael, introduced him to the arts. He’s a competitive athlete with a desire to win, but retains a great sporting attitude. Brad is in charge of the fighters LIHFS enters in to the various competitions around the country.
