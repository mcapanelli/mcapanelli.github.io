Who we are

The Empanada is the ultimate portable meal. They offer a secret world of flavor packed inside a delicious flaky pastry dough--Latin comfort food found throughout Latin America and the Caribbean. Our menu comprises twenty-one varieties of empanadas, seasonal specials, and our Things that make you mmmm to be certain that we can please everyone's taste buds and wallets. Stop in for a quick snack, stay for a sampling of varieties and plates for a complete meal, take home a dozen for your family to enjoy, or order a platter for your next party.
